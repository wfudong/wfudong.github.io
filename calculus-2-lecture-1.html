第 1 讲：课程导论与预备知识

Vectors in Euclidean Space（板书版讲义）

本页内容根据你的 TeX 文件整理，保留核心教学流程与公式，支持直接显示 LaTeX 数学公式。

Board 1：Vectors, Coordinates, and Basic Operations

Euclidean space

$$\R^n=\{(x_1,\dots,x_n)\mid x_i\in\R\}$$ $$\R^2=\{(x,y)\},\qquad \R^3=\{(x,y,z)\}$$

A vector

$$\vect{v}=(a,b)\ \text{in }\R^2,\qquad \vect{v}=(a,b,c)\ \text{in }\R^3$$

Standard basis

$$\vect{e}_1=(1,0,\dots,0),\ \vect{e}_2=(0,1,0,\dots,0),\ \dots,\ \vect{e}_n=(0,\dots,0,1)$$ $$\vect{x}=x_1\vect{e}_1+\cdots+x_n\vect{e}_n$$

Basic operations

$$\vect{u}+\vect{v}=(u_1+v_1,\dots,u_n+v_n)$$ $$c\vect{u}=(cu_1,\dots,cu_n)$$ $$a_1\vect{v}_1+\cdots+a_k\vect{v}_k$$

Quick example

$$(2,-1,3)+(-1,4,0)=(1,3,3)$$

Board 2：Length, Distance, Dot Product, Projection

Norm / Distance / Unit vector

$$\norm{\vect{v}}=\sqrt{v_1^2+\cdots+v_n^2}$$ $$d(P,Q)=\norm{Q-P}$$ $$\widehat{\vect{v}}=\frac{\vect{v}}{\norm{\vect{v}}}\qquad (\vect{v}\neq\vect{0})$$

Dot product and angle

$$\vect{u}\cdot\vect{v}=u_1v_1+\cdots+u_nv_n$$ $$\vect{u}\cdot\vect{v}=\norm{\vect{u}}\norm{\vect{v}}\cos\theta$$ $$\vect{u}\perp\vect{v}\iff \vect{u}\cdot\vect{v}=0$$

Projection

$$\operatorname{proj}_{\vect{v}}\vect{u}=\frac{\vect{u}\cdot\vect{v}}{\vect{v}\cdot\vect{v}}\,\vect{v}$$

Quick example

$$\norm{(3,4)}=5,\qquad \operatorname{proj}_{(1,1)}(3,4)=\frac{7}{2}(1,1)$$

Board 3：Cross Product and Determinants

Cross product in $\R^3$

$$\vect{u}\times\vect{v}=\begin{pmatrix}u_2v_3-u_3v_2\\u_3v_1-u_1v_3\\u_1v_2-u_2v_1\end{pmatrix}$$ $$\vect{u}\times\vect{v}\perp\vect{u},\qquad \vect{u}\times\vect{v}\perp\vect{v}$$ $$\norm{\vect{u}\times\vect{v}}=\norm{\vect{u}}\norm{\vect{v}}\sin\theta$$ $$\text{Area of parallelogram}=\norm{\vect{u}\times\vect{v}}$$

Determinants

$$\det\begin{pmatrix}a & b\\c & d\end{pmatrix}=ad-bc$$ $$\abs{\det(A)}=\text{area scaling factor in }\R^2$$ $$\abs{\det(A)}=\text{volume scaling factor in }\R^3$$ $$\vect{u}\cdot(\vect{v}\times\vect{w})=\det\begin{pmatrix}u_1 & v_1 & w_1\\u_2 & v_2 & w_2\\u_3 & v_3 & w_3\end{pmatrix}$$ $$\det\begin{pmatrix}2 & 1\\1 & 3\end{pmatrix}=5$$

Board 4：Coordinate Change and Matrices as Actions

Coordinate change

$$A=\begin{pmatrix}\vline & \vline\\\vect{v}_1 & \vect{v}_2\\\vline & \vline\end{pmatrix},\qquad \vect{x}_{\text{standard}}=A\vect{x}_{\text{new}}$$ $$\abs{\det(A)}=\text{how unit area or unit volume scales under the coordinate change}$$

Matrix action and multiplication

$$A\vect{x}=\text{a new vector}$$ $$A=\begin{pmatrix}\vline & \vline\\\vect{c}_1 & \vect{c}_2\\\vline & \vline\end{pmatrix},\qquad A\begin{pmatrix}x\\y\end{pmatrix}=x\vect{c}_1+y\vect{c}_2$$ $$(AB)\vect{x}=A(B\vect{x}),\qquad AB\neq BA\ \text{in general}$$ $$\det(AB)=\det(A)\det(B)$$

Board 5：Lines, Planes, and Calculus Connection

$$\vect{r}(t)=\vect{r}_0+t\vect{d}$$ $$\vect{n}\cdot(\vect{r}-\vect{r}_0)=0$$ $$\vect{n}=\vect{u}\times\vect{v}$$ $$\nabla f=\left(\frac{\partial f}{\partial x_1},\dots,\frac{\partial f}{\partial x_n}\right)$$ $$D_{\vect{u}}f=\nabla f\cdot\vect{u}\qquad (\vect{u}\ \text{unit})$$ $$\text{Near a point, a multivariable map behaves like a matrix}$$

Core Board Formulas

$$\vect{x}=x_1\vect{e}_1+\cdots+x_n\vect{e}_n$$ $$\vect{u}+\vect{v}=(u_1+v_1,\dots,u_n+v_n)$$ $$\norm{\vect{v}}=\sqrt{v_1^2+\cdots+v_n^2}$$ $$\vect{u}\cdot\vect{v}=u_1v_1+\cdots+u_nv_n$$ $$\vect{u}\cdot\vect{v}=\norm{\vect{u}}\norm{\vect{v}}\cos\theta$$ $$\operatorname{proj}_{\vect{v}}\vect{u}=\frac{\vect{u}\cdot\vect{v}}{\vect{v}\cdot\vect{v}}\,\vect{v}$$ $$\norm{\vect{u}\times\vect{v}}=\norm{\vect{u}}\norm{\vect{v}}\sin\theta$$ $$\abs{\det(A)}=\text{area/volume scaling}$$ $$\vect{x}_{\text{standard}}=A\vect{x}_{\text{new}}$$ $$(AB)\vect{x}=A(B\vect{x})$$ $$\vect{r}(t)=\vect{r}_0+t\vect{d}$$ $$\vect{n}\cdot(\vect{r}-\vect{r}_0)=0$$ $$D_{\vect{u}}f=\nabla f\cdot\vect{u}$$
