Projects

Ongoing: Spectral Theory of fNLS Soliton/Breather Gases

Status: Active

Current research focuses on the spectral theory of integrable PDEs, especially focusing NLS and related finite-gap structures. This project studies thermodynamic limits, nonlinear dispersion relation integral equations, averaged densities and fluxes, and periodic gas regimes.

Integrable Systems fNLS Spectral Theory

Past: Long-time Asymptotics for AKNS Hierarchy

Status: Completed

This project studies long-time asymptotic behavior of AKNS hierarchy solutions through Riemann-Hilbert formulations and the dbar steepest descent method, with explicit asymptotics and error estimates in decaying, Painlevé, and oscillatory regions.

AKNS Riemann-Hilbert Asymptotics

Past: Electrified Droplets and Free-Boundary Problems

Status: Completed

A side project in complex analysis investigating equilibrium droplet shapes in external electric fields through a free-boundary formulation, leading to a family of physically meaningful solutions.

Complex Analysis Free Boundary Mathematical Physics
